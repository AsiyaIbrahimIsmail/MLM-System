Project Summary

Built for small shops and supermarkets that want a clean way to monitor debts, balances, and day-to-day customer credit records.

Customer Records Track client profiles and balances.
Credit Sales Record products, quantities, and totals.
Payments Update balances after each payment.
Reports Review financial performance quickly.

Main Features

The original PHP application is designed to simplify shop credit workflows and keep financial records organized and easy to review.

Customer Management

Register customers, keep their details, and review their credit profile.

Credit Tracking

Save credit sales with products, quantities, pricing, and calculated totals.

Payment Recording

Record repayments and instantly see the remaining customer balance.

Reports and Analytics

Review collected payments, total credit issued, and outstanding balances.

Secure Access

Use login-based access so business records stay protected inside the system.

Responsive Layout
